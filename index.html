Christopher Caressimo: Living a life of music

About Chris

Hello, My name is Christopher Caressimo. I'm a Drummer and Drum instructor. I have been playing for 14 years of my life, and for the last five years, I have become the former drummer of a band called "Anahata" and now currently spending 2020 working on a new project. I have also been teaching kids from the age of 6 to 18 and also adults for the past 2 years at "The School of Rock" in Rockville Center and Huntington.
