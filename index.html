Music

For most of my life, music has played an important part in it. Whether it was for bonding with friends and family, holidays, passing the time, or helping cope with personal issue, music was always involved. I've grown up listening artist like Frank Sinatra, the Beatles, Led Zeppelin, and Selena.

From High School on, I've developed a taste in heavy music, bands like Opeth, Amon Amarth, and Gojira to name a few. Over the past five years of sharing and playing music with other people, I was introduced into Japanese metal bands such as BabyMetal, the Gazette, Crystal Lake, and Maximum the Hormone, got to travel and play all over the north east, and even got to perform in "Afro Punk" which included a documentary about my last band.

I ended up starting new project band called "[Null]" with my two best friends at the start of 2020 which is still in the works and is not public as of yet.

CLOTHING

While creating a new project with my best friends, we also decided to start up a clothing company called "TrashPlxnet" focused on alternative "e-boy/e-girl" designs and styles. We have been commissioning and working with artists on designs to roll out once we finish our website and stocking up on products.
